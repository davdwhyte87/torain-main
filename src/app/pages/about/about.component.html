Torain Charity Foundation was set up in November 2019. It was set up out of the burning desire to help the most vulnerable people in our communities. It is our genuine belief that corporations should give back to the society that accommodate and support them. It burns our hearts to see people hungry, not being able to take care of their Children's educational needs or treat malaria which is endemic in our societies..This is why we took it upon ourselves at Torain Charity Foundation to help as much as we can to alleviate the pain and sufferings of the most vulnerable persons in our communities by investing in malaria treatment, food security and education promotion

CHAIRMAN

Charles Ihenacho is a Pharmacist by profession. He is a research scientist with several medicinal formulations that are widely used today in Nigeria and beyond in the treatment of Malaria, Fertility, Cancer management, Diabetes, etc. He is also a successful business man and is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Torain International. He is a humanitarian at heart and is a proponent of Corporate Social Responsibility as a way of organizations giving back to communities that accommodate them. He is the main financier of a Non Governmental Organization called Girl Child Foundation which supports the advancement of the girl child developement. He is married and has three beautiful girls.
