Our Vision

Our vision is to improve the quality of living standards in our societies, eradicate malaria and other communicable diseases and enable less privileged children to have access to education and good educational materials.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eradicate poor standard of living conditions, access to quality Educational materials and health product to eradicate malaria and boost inmmune system amongst the most vulnerables persons in our society.

Torain Charity Foundation is a charity organization that directly supports vulnerable communities, with a focus on rural areas. We distribute food, educational materials,, and health products to improve living standards and empower individuals of all ages.

Why We Do What We Do

Our core value at Torain
foundation is to be known humanists and to live a life filled with empathy, love, passion and support for every human in our societies irrespective of ethnicity, religion, gender and age.

Who We Are

Torain Charity Foundation was born out of a deep desire to help those who are most vulnerable in our communities. We believe that it's our responsibility to give back to the society that supports us. Through investments in malaria treatment, food security, and education, we strive to make a difference in the lives of those who need it most. Join us in our mission to alleviate the pain and suffering of those in need.
